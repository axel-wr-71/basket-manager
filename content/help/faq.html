How do I create an account? +

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click the "Create Account" button on the homepage
  2. Enter your email address and choose a secure password
  3. Create your username and team name
  4. Select your country from the dropdown menu
  5. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  6. Click "Create Account" to complete registration

You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once confirmed, you can log in and start managing your team!

Is Elite Buzzer League free to play? +

Yes! Elite Buzzer League is completely free to play. You can enjoy all core gameplay features without any cost.

We offer optional premium features and cosmetic items for those who want to enhance their experience, but these are never required to compete or enjoy the game.

What system requirements are needed? +

Elite Buzzer League runs in your web browser and has minimal requirements:

  • Browser: Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
  • Internet: Stable internet connection (broadband recommended)
  • Screen: Minimum 1024x768 resolution
  • JavaScript: Must be enabled in your browser

The game is designed to work on desktop computers, laptops, and tablets. Mobile support is coming soon!

üéÆ Gameplay

How do I train my players? +

Player training is essential for improving your team:

  1. Navigate to the "Training" tab from your main menu
  2. Select which players you want to train
  3. Choose training focus: Shooting, Defense, Conditioning, or Playmaking
  4. Allocate training intensity (light, medium, heavy)
  5. Click "Start Training" to begin the session

Tips: Training too intensely can lead to player fatigue. Balance training with rest periods for optimal results.

How does the transfer market work? +

The transfer market is where you buy and sell players:

  • Bidding: Place bids on available players
  • Auction: Some players are sold through timed auctions
  • Direct Offers: Make offers to other managers for their players
  • Free Agents: Sign unattached players

Each player has a market value based on age, skills, and performance. Prices fluctuate based on supply and demand.

How often do games occur? +

Game schedule depends on your league:

  • Regular Season: 2-3 games per week (varies by league)
  • Playoffs: Daily games during playoff weeks
  • Cup Competitions: Weekly knockout matches
  • Friendlies: Can be scheduled at any time

Games are simulated automatically at scheduled times. You can set your lineup and strategy before each game.

üèÄ Teams & Players

How many players can I have on my team? +

Team roster limits:

  • Active Roster: 12-15 players (varies by league rules)
  • Reserve Squad: Up to 5 additional players
  • Youth Team: Unlimited players under age 21

You must maintain at least 10 active players at all times. Exceeding roster limits may result in automatic player releases or penalties.

How do I improve my team's chemistry? +

Team chemistry affects performance significantly. Improve it by:

  1. Consistent Lineups: Keep your starting five together
  2. Training Together: Group training sessions
  3. Team Meetings: Hold weekly team meetings
  4. Player Personalities: Sign players with compatible personalities
  5. Winning Games: Success boosts chemistry

Chemistry ranges from 0-100. Higher chemistry provides performance bonuses in games.

Can I change my team name? +

Yes, with limitations:

  • Free name change once per season
  • Additional changes cost 500 EBL Coins
  • Cannot change to inappropriate or offensive names
  • Cannot impersonate other teams or real-world organizations

To change your team name, go to Account Settings ‚Üí Team Management ‚Üí Change Team Name.

üîê Account & Security

How do I reset my password? +

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Log in with your new credentials

Password reset links expire after 1 hour for security. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

Can I delete my account? +

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. To delete your account:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Account Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Security
  3. Scroll to "Delete Account" section
  4. Enter your password to confirm
  5. Click "Permanently Delete Account"

Warning: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your teams, players, and game progress. This action cannot be undone.

How do I enable two-factor authentication? +

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to Account Settings ‚Üí Security
  3. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  4. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  5. Enter the 6-digit code from your app
  6. Save your backup codes in a secure location

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA to protect your account from unauthorized access.
